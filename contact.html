Contact & Booking

General Bookings

If you would like to book a session please email us at cameracapturephotos@gmail.com including location (address) time and date, general purpose, contact method(s), and preferred cameras (check home page)

Deposit

If the location of the photoshoot is greater than 30km away from the intersection between Leslie & Elgin Mills East (defined by the coordinates (43.898552457697996, -79.39582126954045)) then we may ask for a CAD $10 deposit plus $2 per kilometer (kilometer rounded up) (E-Transfer to jettmuu@gmail.com only after I confirm availability and if you have to do so or not)

Cancellation Policy

If the event is cancelled at least 48 hours before the event by notifying cameracapturephotos@gmail.com then no charge will be needed but if it is cancelled within 48 hours of the event then a CAD $1 charge will be applied.
